A can of home (2019)

In this series, I imagined what it would be like to build a home out of cans. In Hong Kong, the price of housing is so high that it is common for locals to live in crammed homes. This series is meant to be a light-hearted capture of the bitter situation.

Created in 2019 with Adobe Illustrator.
